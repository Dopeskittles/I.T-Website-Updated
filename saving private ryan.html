Movie Review :


Saving Private Ryan is a true story about the 2nd Ranger Battalion and their mission to go behind enemy lines in order to locate Private James Ryan. Unknown to Private Ryan, he is the last living sibling out of his four brothers. In order to save Mrs. Ryan feeling the pain of losing another son, Captain John Miller and his soldiers are sent on a special assignment to find Ryan and bring him home safely.
