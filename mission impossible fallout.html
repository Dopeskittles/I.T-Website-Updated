Movie Review :


Mission Impossible Fallout is set two years after Ethan Hunt captured Solomon Lane. The remainders of the Syndicate have formed a new terrorist group known as the Apostles. Under the leadership of a mysterious man only known as John Lark, the group plans to launch a simultaneous nuclear attack on three different locations. In order to save the world from this catastrophic disaster Ethan and his team must recover the three plutonium cores before it's too late.
