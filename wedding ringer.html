Movie Review :


The Wedding Ringer features Doug Harris, a socially awkward business man who's about to get married to the girl of his dreams. Doug being a man who focused on his carrer has no friends to be his groomsmen. Desperate to make a good impression for his wedding, Doug enlists the services of Jimmy Callahan the owner of Best Man Inc. Jimmy struggles to pull off the "Golden Tux" for Doug's wedding by creating a lifetime of friends and adventures he never had.
