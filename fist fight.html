Fist Fight

Movie Information :


Genres

Comedy


Director

Richie Keen


Rating Information

Ages 16+


Staring

Charlie Day, Ice Cube, Tracey Morgan, Jillian Bell, Dean Norris, Christina Hendricks, Kumail Nanijani, Dennis Hysbert, Kym Whitley

Movie Review :


Amid the pranks taking place on the last day of school, English teacher Andy Campbell incurs the wrath of History teacher Ron Strickland. Ron challenges Andy to a fist fight after school for ratting him out resulting in him being fired. Andy tries to desperately escape his inevitable encounter with Ron in the parking lot. The two will clash in a one on one fight with the whole school onlooking.
