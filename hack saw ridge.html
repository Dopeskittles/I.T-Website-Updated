Movie Review :


Hacksaw Ridge is the true story of how American Army Medic Desmond T. Doss saved 75 soldiers during the battle of Okinawa. Desmond, due to his religious beliefs refused to bare arms and take the life of enemy soldiers. Despite his pacifist stance he was rewarded the Congressional Medal of Honor for his selfless act of heroism and bravery.
