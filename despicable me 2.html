Movie Review :


Everyone's favourite supervillan Gru is back in Despicable Me 2 this time adjusting to family life. As if the challenges and struggles of being a father were not enough, he is called upon by the A.V.L to track down a new criminal mastermind. Returning to his old life of gadgets and convoluted plans, Gru along with his new partner Lucy team up to investigate a series of elaborate crimes. This is done in an effort to save the world from this new unknown threat.
