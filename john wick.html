Movie Review :


John Wick 2 embodies the age old statement that your past actions come back to haunt you. This is the case when Italian gangster Santino D'Antonio shows up at John's doorstep with a golden marker. This marker forces John to abandon his quiet civilian life and once again become the deadly assassin we all know. The plot begins with John travelling to Rome where he has to take out D'Antonio's sister and fulfill his marker.
