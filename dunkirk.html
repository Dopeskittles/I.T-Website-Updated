Movie Review :


This movie is based upon a true story about the mass evacuation of 330,000 French, British, Belgian and Dutch soldiers from the French port town of Dunkirk. The invading German army have pushed back against the allied forces. The only way out is via the ocean. Britain sends its Navy along with every other civilian ship they can muster in order to bring their army home. The story is told through many different perspectives giving the audience a full insight into the harsh realities of war.
