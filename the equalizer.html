Movie Review :


One would take a look at Robert McCall and fail to realize that he's a former special services commando. To escape his old life Robert faked his own death and started over living a more peaceful existence. This peace and brief respite is cut short when members of a russian gang attack a young girl. Robert being a man that cannot stand for injustice against others comes out of his self-imposed retirement. Using the knowledge and unique skill set of his past, Robert sets out to enact vengance upon those who prey on the innocent.
