Movie Review :


If you're a fan of Marlon Wayans some of the material in this movie feels recycled, if not you may enjoy this film titled Sextuplets. The main storyline of the movie is set around Alan, a soon to be father, who thought he was an only child. When searching for his birth mother, Alan soon discovered that he has five other siblings that he never know about. Alan then sets out on a road trip to find his siblings and bring everyone together.
