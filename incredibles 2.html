Movie Review :


In a world where people using superpowers are still illegal the Parr family still do their part to ensure the safety of citizens. The storyline of Incredibles 2 begins when two wealthy siblings approach Elastigirl asking her to become the face of a new campaign. In hopes of changing the public image of superheroes Elastigirl accepts. This decision results in Mr. Incredible leading a more domestic life of staying home and taking care of the kids. Things do not remain quiet for long, as family unites together to stop a new villian known as Screenslaver.
