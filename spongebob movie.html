Movie Review :


Being the latest movie in the SpongeBob SquarePants franchise, Sponge on the Run provides good family friendly content that everyone can enjoy. The movie's plot starts when Plankton hatches a new plan to steal the Krabby Patty secret formula. This leads to Gary being snail napped and taken to The Lost City of Atlantic City. SpongeBob and his bestfriend Patrick then set out on an adventure to rescue Gary and bring him home.
