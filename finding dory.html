Movie Review :


Your favourite cast of characters are back in the long awaited sequel titled Finding Dory. This movie is centered around your favourite and forgetful blue tang fish Dory. The story begins when Dory starts having flashbacks of her childhood and her parents. Eventually, Dory along with her friends Marlin and Nemo embark on an adventure across the ocean to find her parents. Along the way they were joined by old and new characters who aided them in completing her quest.
